What is Water Runoff?

Surface runoff is precipitation that runs off the landscape The oceans are kept full by precipitation and also by runoff and discharge from rivers and the ground.
A simple way to put it is: precipitation falls on the land, flows overland (runoff), and runs into rivers, which then empty into the oceans.

What is Bycatch?

Fishermen sometimes catch and discard animals they do not want, cannot sell, or are not allowed to keep. This is collectively known as “bycatch.”
Bycatch can be fish, but also includes other animals such as dolphins, whales, sea turtles, and seabirds that become hooked or entangled in fishing gear.


Some Disturbing Facts About Dolphins

Contrary to their reputation for friendliness, dolphins can display aggressive behavior towards humans and other marine species.
While interactions with humans are often positive, there have been documented cases of dolphins exhibiting aggressive tendencies, including biting, ramming, and even sexual assault.
These incidents serve as a reminder that despite their intelligence, dolphins are wild animals with unpredictable behavior patterns.
